# Frontier 10052 — Factions

## Major powers

- **Terran Continuity Authority:** guardian of heritage, continuity, ceremony, and regulated legitimacy.
- **Sirius Corporate Compact:** efficient, contract-driven, innovative, and extractive.
- **Frontier Freeholds:** independent communities with strong local identity and uneven capacity.
- **Kuiper Syndicates:** logistics, labor, smuggling, protection, and services beyond formal law.
- **Andromedan Federation:** functioning democratic society, attractive migration destination, and tightly regulated polity.

## Galactic Gate Authority

The GGA operates Builder Gates under a constrained neutrality. It controls schedules, manifests, safety rules, activation economics, and access procedures but cannot manufacture Gates or fully explain them.

## Other powers

The declining United Human Systems, Assurance Houses of Procyon, Advent Churches, Builder cults, Stillness Communion, Plainlife Bloc, Praetor mercenary lines, pirate networks, Unison Republics, and Threshold migration industry influence regional play.

## Reputation model

Track separately:

- legal status by jurisdiction;
- faction standing;
- personal relationships;
- commercial trust;
- insurance risk;
- criminal credibility;
- public notoriety.

A hero in one system may be a criminal in another. News of actions propagates physically and arrives at different times.

## Simulation

Factions possess goals, resources, territories, institutions, relationships, current leadership, internal divisions, and reactions to events. They issue contracts, enforce law, move assets, spread narratives, and remember favors and harm.

## Design rule

No faction is uniformly good or evil. Each must provide real benefits, impose real costs, contain disagreement, and offer at least one credible reason for loyalty and one credible reason for resistance.
