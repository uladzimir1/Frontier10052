# Frontier 10052 — Appendix

The appendix contains canonical background and implementation references supporting the numbered production specifications.

## Canon source set

The project’s extended setting bible includes:

- **Galactic Atlas:** Sol Heritage Zone, Core Belt, Compact Space, Lyra Reach, Meridian Worlds, Old Battlegrounds, Freehold Rim, Silent Margins, Threshold, and published knowledge of Andromeda.
- **Faction Compendium:** major factions, Galactic Gate Authority, declining United Human Systems, minor powers, criminal networks, faiths, and migration institutions.
- **Technology Bible:** metric drive, Gate network, communications, artificial intelligence, cybernetics, medicine, weapons, fabrication, and Builder technology.
- **Historical Figures:** people whose actions shaped the current political and technological order.
- **History:** the chronology from 2026 to 10052, including Solar expansion, first stellar age, Colonial Wars, Great Union, Builders, Gate Wars, Golden Millennium, first contact, Border Era, and Age of Decline.
- **Colonial Wars:** detailed history and enduring wreck, legal, cultural, and faction consequences.
- **Culture:** languages, trade-cant, calendars, food, entertainment, fashion, etiquette, faith, and regional daily life.

## Non-negotiable canon

1. No faster-than-light communication.
2. Warp moves ships and cannot operate near strong gravity wells.
3. Humanity can operate but cannot build or fully understand Builder Gates.
4. Gate travel remains exceptionally expensive.
5. Earth is sacred but diminished; Mars and Pluto are major opening centers.
6. Andromeda subsidizes inward migration but not ordinary return.
7. Technology always has material, energy, maintenance, legal, or institutional limits.
8. Crew are persistent people, not interchangeable bonuses.
9. Combat is avoidable and consequential.
10. The player never becomes immune to distance, law, maintenance, scarcity, or relationships.

## Recommended additional appendix files

Future additions should include a glossary, stable-ID registry, content schemas, timeline summary, faction relationship matrix, system catalogue, cargo catalogue, ship-module catalogue, accessibility checklist, and save migration log.
